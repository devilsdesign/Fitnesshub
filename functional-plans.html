Plan 1: Basic Functional Training

Goal:

Improve overall functional strength and endurance with basic movement patterns.

Frequency:

3 days per week (e.g., Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

Day 1: Full-Body Functional Movements

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Superman Hold: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Core and Stability

  • Side Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Bird Dogs: 3 sets of 15 reps per side
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 3: Lower Body Strength and Power

  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Single-leg Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Step-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Plan 2: Intermediate Functional Training

Goal:

Increase strength and functional capacity with more challenging movements and added resistance.

Frequency:

4 days per week (e.g., Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday)

Day 1: Full-Body Strength and Mobility

  • Goblet Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups (Elevated): 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Walking Lunges with Dumbbells: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Plank to Push-up: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Superman Hold: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 2: Cardio and Core

  • Running: 15 minutes (Intervals of 1 min sprint, 2 min walk)
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists with Medicine Ball: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Plank with Arm Reach: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 3: Power and Plyometrics

  • Box Jumps: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Jump Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Broad Jumps: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 4: Functional Upper Body

  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Renegade Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps per side
  • Single-arm Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps per arm
  • Dumbbell Thrusters: 3 sets of 10 reps

Plan 3: Advanced Functional Training

Goal:

Maximize functional strength and power using advanced exercises with heavier weights and higher intensity.

Frequency:

5 days per week (e.g., Monday to Friday)

Day 1: Lower Body Strength and Power

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Jump Squats: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Weighted Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 2: Upper Body Strength and Endurance

  • Pull-ups (Weighted if possible): 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank to Push-up: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 3: Full-Body Functional Movements

  • Power Cleans: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Box Jumps: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Push Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 4: Cardio and Agility

  • Agility Ladder Drills: 3 sets
  • Sprints: 10 sets of 20 seconds on, 40 seconds off
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 5: Core and Mobility

  • Side Plank with Hip Dip: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Dragon Flag: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Stretching and Foam Rolling: 10 minutes

Plan 4: Functional Conditioning and Speed

Goal:

Focus on explosive power, endurance, and speed with high-intensity functional training.

Frequency:

5 days per week (e.g., Monday to Friday)

Day 1: Power and Strength

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Overhead Squats: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Weighted Box Jumps: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Push-ups with Clap: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2: Speed and Agility

  • Shuttle Runs: 5 sets of 20 yards
  • Side-to-Side Jumps: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Lateral Bounds: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Plank to Push-up: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 3: Full-Body Conditioning

  • Jumping Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Push Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Thrusters: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps

Day 4: Plyometrics and Cardio

  • Jump Squats: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Sprints: 10 sets of 20 seconds on, 40 seconds off

Day 5: Core Strength and Flexibility

  • Dragon Flags: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Side Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds per side
  • V-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Yoga and Stretching: 15 minutes
