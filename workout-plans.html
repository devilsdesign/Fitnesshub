Plan 1: Beginner Bodybuilding Workout

Goal:

Build a foundation of strength and muscle mass while learning the basics of resistance training.

Frequency:

3 days per week (e.g., Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

Day 1: Upper Body

  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Day 2: Lower Body

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Dumbbell Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Leg Press (if available): 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Dumbbell Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 3: Full Body

  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Dumbbell Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Pull-ups/Assisted Pull-ups: 3 sets of 5-10 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 12 reps

Plan 2: Intermediate Bodybuilding Workout

Goal:

Increase muscle mass and strength while improving workout intensity.

Frequency:

4 days per week (e.g., Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday)

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

  • Flat Bench Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Cable Flyes: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extensions: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Tricep Pushdowns: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 2: Back and Biceps

  • Pull-ups (Weighted if possible): 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Bent-over Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Single-arm Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 3: Legs and Abs

  • Squats (Barbell or Dumbbell): 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Cable Crunches: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 4: Shoulders and Abs

  • Overhead Barbell Press: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Front Raises: 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Shrugs: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps (10 per side)

Plan 3: Advanced Bodybuilding Workout

Goal:

Maximize muscle hypertrophy and strength through advanced techniques and heavy lifting.

Frequency:

5 days per week (e.g., Monday to Friday)

Day 1: Chest (Heavy Day)

  • Flat Barbell Bench Press: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Chest Dips: 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Cable Crossovers: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups (Weighted if possible): 3 sets of 20 reps

Day 2: Back (Heavy Day)

  • Deadlifts: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Pull-ups (Weighted if possible): 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • T-Bar Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Face Pulls: 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Day 3: Legs (Heavy Day)

  • Squats: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 12 reps

Day 4: Shoulders and Arms

  • Overhead Barbell Press: 4 sets of 6-8 reps
  • Arnold Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curls: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Skull Crushers: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 5: Abs and Cardio

  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute

Plan 4: Strength Focus

Goal:

Build maximal strength with heavy lifting and low reps.

Frequency:

4 days per week (e.g., Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday)

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

  • Bench Press: 5 sets of 5 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Close-grip Bench Press: 4 sets of 5 reps
  • Skull Crushers: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Overhead Tricep Extension: 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 2: Back and Biceps

  • Deadlifts: 5 sets of 5 reps
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Pull-ups (Weighted if possible): 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 6 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 8 reps

Day 3: Legs

  • Squats: 5 sets of 5 reps
  • Leg Press: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 3 sets of 8 reps
  • Seated Calf Raises: 4 sets of 12 reps

Day 4: Shoulders and Abs

  • Overhead Press: 5 sets of 5 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Barbell Shrugs: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Hanging Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
