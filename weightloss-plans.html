Plan 1: Beginner Weight Loss Workout

Goal:

Lose weight and build endurance through cardio and full-body strength exercises.

Frequency:

3 days per week (e.g., Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

Day 1: Full-Body Circuit

  • Jumping Jacks: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Plank: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Cardio and Abs

  • Brisk Walk or Jog: 20 minutes
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 45 seconds

Day 3: Lower Body and Cardio

  • Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Step-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Jump Rope: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Plan 2: Intermediate Weight Loss Workout

Goal:

Increase fat burn with more advanced cardio and resistance training.

Frequency:

4 days per week (e.g., Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday)

Day 1: Cardio and Abs

  • Running (Treadmill or Outdoors): 20 minutes
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 25 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Full-Body Strength Training

  • Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 3: HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

  • Burpees: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off (Repeat 5 times)
  • Jump Squats: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off (Repeat 5 times)
  • Jumping Jacks: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off (Repeat 5 times)
  • Mountain Climbers: 30 seconds on, 30 seconds off (Repeat 5 times)

Day 4: Lower Body and Cardio

  • Bodyweight Squats: 4 sets of 12 reps
  • Step-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Jump Rope: 3 sets of 2 minutes
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds

Plan 3: Advanced Weight Loss Workout

Goal:

Maximize fat loss with intense cardio and strength training.

Frequency:

5 days per week (e.g., Monday to Friday)

Day 1: HIIT Cardio

  • Burpees: 5 sets of 20 seconds
  • Jump Lunges: 5 sets of 20 seconds
  • High Knees: 5 sets of 30 seconds
  • Jumping Jacks: 5 sets of 30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers: 5 sets of 30 seconds

Day 2: Lower Body Strength

  • Squats: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Step-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps per leg
  • Calf Raises: 4 sets of 15 reps

Day 3: Upper Body Strength

  • Push-ups: 4 sets of 15 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Bicep Curls: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 4: Full-Body Circuit

  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 5: Cardio and Abs

  • Running: 20 minutes
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute

Plan 4: High-Intensity Weight Loss Workout

Goal:

Focus on high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and cardio to shred fat quickly.

Frequency:

6 days per week (e.g., Monday to Saturday)

Day 1: HIIT Cardio

  • Sprints: 30 seconds sprint, 30 seconds walk (Repeat 10 times)
  • Jump Rope: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 20 seconds
  • High Knees: 3 sets of 1 minute

Day 2: Lower Body and Abs

  • Squats: 4 sets of 15 reps
  • Step-ups: 4 sets of 12 reps per leg
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Day 3: Upper Body

  • Push-ups: 4 sets of 20 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 12 reps

Day 4: Full-Body Circuit

  • Jump Squats: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 45 seconds
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Burpees: 3 sets of 20 seconds

Day 5: Cardio and Abs

  • Running: 30 minutes
  • Russian Twists: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • Leg Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Plank: 3 sets of 1 minute
